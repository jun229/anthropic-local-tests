TITLE: Community Partner Marketer
DEPARTMENT: Business Operations & Strategy

---INTRO---
The Uniswap Labs team is building products to unlock value through universal exchange. We envision a future where digital economies flourish, and markets are transparent, global, and equitable. Our products – the Uniswap Interface, the Uniswap Mobile App, and the Uniswap Extension – are powered by the Uniswap Protocol.  The protocol is the largest onchain marketplace, with billions of dollars in weekly volume across thousands of tokens on Ethereum and 8+ additional chains. When you join Uniswap, you become part of a team that's reshaping the way value flows on the internet. The Uniswap marketing team is looking for a dynamic, self-starting Community Partner Marketer to join the community team! You’ll help build deeper relationships with many subsets of the Uniswap and larger DeFi community...

---RESPONSIBILITIES---
- Relationship Management and Community Engagement:
- Develop and manage 1:1 relationships with key  Uniswap community stakeholders and swapper segments, ensuring personalized engagement and their long-term success with Uniswap
- Manage and nurture a portfolio of existing DeFi community and partner relationships.
- Build and maintain a CRM of these users, ecosystem partners and Uniswap advocates
- Communication and Digital Channel Management:
- Develop creative approaches to maintaining open lines of communication with each segment of the community.
- Oversee digital channels/forums (Slack, Telegram, Discord, etc.) to engage with these different  subsets of Uniswap community members.
- Facilitate communication and regular updates between partners and internal stakeholders to ensure alignment and transparency.
- Planning, Collaboration and Marketing:
- Collaborate with content marketers and social teams to develop and execute creative marketing campaigns that align with broader company objectives.

- 5+ years of work experience with 3+ years of community building work
- 1+ years experience facilitating of digital communication groups
- Be a no-task-too-big, no-task-too-small partner in crime to the Uniswap community and marketing team
- Crypto native, specifically in the DeFi ecosystem with some technical understanding of the Crypto/DeFi ecosystem, eg follows blockchain tech advancements and market trends; Able to explain token standards and how different DeFi platforms work at a high level
- Highly organized systems thinker
- Self-starter who prefers operating without a clearly defined playbook
- Track record of executing on large projects start-to-finish
- Comfortable with change, ability to adapt to new information
- NYC based, but willing to travel, up to 15-20%

---NICE_TO_HAVES---
- Relationships with key builders and leaders in crypto / web3
- Deep in different crypto communities, experience with Discord & Telegram

- Has successfully built & managed relationships on behalf of their company
