---INTRO---
The Uniswap Labs team is building products to unlock value through universal exchange. We envision a future where digital economies flourish, and markets are transparent, global, and equitable. Our products – the Uniswap Interface, the Uniswap Mobile App, and the Uniswap Extension – are powered by the Uniswap Protocol.  The protocol is the largest onchain marketplace, with billions of dollars in weekly volume across thousands of tokens on Ethereum and 8+ additional chains. When you join Uniswap, you become part of a team that's reshaping the way value flows on the internet. We’re looking for an enthusiastic, collaborative, and communicative senior product marketer to spearhead user research, develop detailed user personas, and drive the go-to-market strategy for key products. ---RESPONSIBILITIES---
- User Research:

Design and Execute Research: Lead planning and conduct both qualitative and quantitative research initiatives to develop and continuously refine detailed Uniswap retail user personas.
Collaborate with Data Teams: Partner with research and data science teams to gain deep insights into user segments, monitoring and influencing user behavior and product usage through comprehensive analytics.

Market Research:

Competitive Analysis: Regularly monitor and research competitive products, value propositions, and market positioning, generating weekly reports to inform strategic decisions.
Market Positioning: Collaborate with product and research teams to perform competitive benchmarking and develop effective market positioning strategies.

Product Feedback:

User Feedback Collection: Gather and analyze user feedback obtained through research to inform product development.
Roadmap Prioritization: Work closely with the Product team to prioritize features and enhancements on the product roadmap, ensuring feedback is effectively communicated to product, design, and engineering teams with clarity and credibility. Unblocks self and others as required to move projects forward.

Marketing Strategies:

Strategy Development: Create comprehensive marketing and go-to-market strategies for large product upgrades and releases. Define target audience, positioning and messaging - rooted in clear objectives and insights. Able to delegate tasks to team members and partners, to focus on highest impact strategy work.
Collaboration with Channel Owners: Partner with channel owners to develop and implement acquisition and retention strategies that align with the high level strategy for each release
Content Creation: Develop targeted marketing collateral for various touchpoints, such as events, social media channels, and community platforms like Telegram.
