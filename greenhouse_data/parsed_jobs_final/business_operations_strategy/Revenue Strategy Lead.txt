---INTRO---
The Uniswap Labs team is building products to unlock value through universal exchange. We envision a future where digital economies flourish, and markets are transparent, global, and equitable. Our products – the Uniswap Interface, the Uniswap Mobile App, and the Uniswap Extension – are powered by the Uniswap Protocol and Uniswap Platform. The protocol is the largest onchain marketplace, with billions of dollars in weekly volume across thousands of tokens on Ethereum and 8+ additional chains. When you join Uniswap, you become part of a team that's reshaping the way value flows on the internet. The Chief of Staff is a strategic and operational leader, supporting the Chief Operating Officer (COO) in driving company-wide planning, cross-functional coordination, and executive-level initiatives...

---RESPONSIBILITIES---
- Strategic Planning & Execution
- Lead annual and quarterly planning, ensuring all teams are aligned with strategic goals.
- Track and monitor goals/roadmaps to ensure alignment with Uniswap mission & strategy.
- Facilitate cross-functional coordination, resolving dependencies and bottlenecks.
- Collaborate with department heads to drive execution and accountability.
- Be a positive force for operational excellence, improving company and cross-functional velocity through collaboration, communication and process
- Executive Support & Communication
- Prepare the COO for policy and business development meetings, offering market and partner context, data + insights and key talking points.
- Represent the COO in meetings, ensuring their voice and perspective are accurately reflected.
- Draft communications and documents on behalf of the COO, maintaining consistency in tone and messaging.
