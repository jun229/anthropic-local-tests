TITLE: Social Media Specialist
DEPARTMENT: Business Operations & Strategy

---INTRO---
We’re looking for an enthusiastic, self-motivated Social Media Specialist to reach audiences online and creatively tell the story of the transformative possibilities of crypto, Defi, and Web3. The Social Media Specialist will collaborate with the team to launch social campaigns, creating content across all platforms — including Twitter, TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. This role will collaborate with internal teams including community, marketing, product, engineering, design, and research. The Social Media Specialist will manage multiple social campaigns simultaneously, use data driven strategies to reach users, and craft thoughtful strategies that will serve unique audiences. ---RESPONSIBILITIES---
- Execute our overall social strategy from concepting ideas, to coordinating among teams, to executing, to creatively bringing messages to life
- Own content creation and execution from start to finish for all platforms, in coordination with multiple teams and while maintaining Uniswap's distinct brand tone
- Coordinate across the organization to develop a deep understanding of product offerings and help plan innovative go-to-market plans for new announcements
- Develop social media calendar for all platforms, which is integrated with communications and product timelines
- Cultivate relationships with users across platforms
- Identify growth opportunities across platforms
- Run analytics and develop insights that are relevant to our core audiences and integrate findings into engagement planning

---REQUIREMENTS---
- 1-3 years of experience running multiple social media platforms for an organization or agency
- Obsessed with crypto, DeFi, and Web3 as transformative opportunities
- Proven ability to use social media to maintain or build a brand, with experience in everything from explainer threads, to product marketing, to playful memes
- Excellent copywriting skills and ability to develop and adapt tone of voice
- Deep understanding of social media platforms, especially Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube
- Experience with social analytics tools like Hootsuite to inform decision making
- Graphic design experience and familiarity with basic design tools to create image content
