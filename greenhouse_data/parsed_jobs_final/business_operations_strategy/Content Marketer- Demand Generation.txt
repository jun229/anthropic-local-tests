TITLE: Content Marketer- Demand Generation
DEPARTMENT: Business Operations & Strategy

---INTRO---
As an integral marketing team member, you will be responsible for planning and executing cross-channel marketing campaigns to drive user acquisition, engagement, and retention for our apps. You will work closely with a paid-focused growth marketer, partner marketing, and social teams. This role requires a strong understanding of web2 and web3 marketing strategies, exceptional writing skills, experience with product positioning, and the ability to drive creative messaging and visual assets. This role is responsible for delivering and maintaining a high bar of brand quality and brand alignment across all campaign activities.

---RESPONSIBILITIES---
- Create and implement brand and user acquisition integrated marketing campaigns that align with go-to-market strategy and drive towards our growth and brand awareness goals.
- Produce compelling and engaging content for various marketing channels including social media, emails, infographics, blog posts, advertisements. Write punchy, brand aligned copy to align with campaigns, events and activations.
- Own, in partnership with Design, company website, product pages and campaign landing pages from a marketing perspective.
- Collaborate with and provide artistic and creative direction to Design team partners to create effective marketing materials and campaigns
- Be the steward of how our brand shows up in marketing assets across integrated campaigns, website landing pages and paid marketing. Provide creative direction and identify opportunities to up level brand quality and story telling across all marketing activities.
- Manage, write and grow monthly email newsletter. Determine appropriate target audience, frequency and goals.
- Monitor and analyze campaign performance across multiple channels, including email, social media, and web. Craft and adjust strategy based on performance.
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices to ensure our marketing efforts remain current and effective

---REQUIREMENTS---
- 3+ years of experience in marketing, with a focus on consumer apps; bonus fintech or crypto applications
- Proven track record of creating strong copy writing, creative collaboration with design teams and effective story telling
- Provide track record of developing and executing successful marketing campaigns
- Strong project management skills and attention to detail
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Ability to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams
- Minimum full-time salary of $130,000-$145,000. Disclosure in accordance with New York City's Pay Transparency Law. Full Time employees at Uniswap Labs are also eligible for other compensation elements, including equity, tokens, and benefits, dependent on the position type.
