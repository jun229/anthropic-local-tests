---RESPONSIBILITIES---
- In partnership with HRBP and People Ops, support in the following areas for all People programs. Programs include, feedback and engagement cycles, learning & development, team events & on sites, compensation, DEI, employee relations, and team design.
- Project Coordination: Assist in the planning and coordination of People team activities, ensuring all tasks are completed on time and within scope.
- Communication: Maintain clear and effective communication with team members and stakeholders, providing updates on project status and any issues that arise.
- Task Management: Track and manage project-related tasks, ensuring team members are participating in programs and alert HRBP if engagement is low.
- Documentation: Prepare and maintain project documentation, including meeting notes, progress reports, and project plans.
- Support Senior Managers: Provide support to senior project managers by helping with administrative tasks, data collection, and report generation.
- Resource Scheduling: Assist in scheduling resources, including personnel and equipment, to ensure project tasks are adequately staffed and equipped.
- Workshop Coordination: Organize and coordinate sessions for team members, in partnership with HRBP & People Ops.
- Increase impact through data: Help identify potential project issues and areas of improvement through data, and suggest strategies to improve the program.
- Continuous Improvement: Gather feedback from team members and stakeholders to identify areas for improvement in project processes and workflows.

---REQUIREMENTS---
- Ability to analyze data and generate reports.
- Critical thinking skills to identify issues and recommend solutions.
- Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to build relationships and work collaboratively in a team environment.
- Excellent organizational and time management abilities.
- Attention to detail and ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously.
- Strong problem-solving abilities, including identifying issues, proposing solutions, and implementing corrective actions.

---NICE_TO_HAVES---
- Entry-level project management certification such as CAPM (Certified Associate in Project Management) and/or HR-related certification such as SHRM-CP or PHR.
- Familiarity with HRIS and other HR-related software (eg Lattice).
