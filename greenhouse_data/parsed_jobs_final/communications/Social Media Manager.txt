TITLE: Social Media Manager
DEPARTMENT: Communications

---INTRO---
We’re looking for an enthusiastic, self-motivated Social Media Manager to reach audiences online and creatively tell the story of the transformative possibilities of crypto, Defi, and Web3. The Social Media Manager will lead and launch social campaigns, owning all components of content from end-to-end across all platforms, including Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. This role will collaborate with internal teams including community, marketing, product, engineering, design, and research. The SMM will manage multiple social campaigns simultaneously, use data driven strategies to reach users, and craft thoughtful strategies that will serve unique audiences. ---RESPONSIBILITIES---
- Drive our overall social strategy from concepting ideas, to coordinating among teams, to executing, to creatively bringing messages to life
- Own content creation and execution from start to finish for all platforms, in coordination with multiple teams and while maintaining Uniswap's distinct brand tone

- Cultivate relationships with users across platforms
- Identify growth opportunities across platforms

---REQUIREMENTS---
- 3-5 years of experience running multiple social media platforms for an organization or agency
- Informed and passionate about crypto, DeFi, and Web3 as transformative opportunities

- Excellent copywriting skills and ability to develop and adapt tone of voice
- Deep understanding of social media platforms, especially Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube
- Experience with social analytics tools like Sprout Social to inform decision making
- Graphic design experience and familiarity with basic design tools to create image content
